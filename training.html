Online Training

Onpne training is useful for a number of reasons:

Participants can attend a course at anytime from anywhere and update themselves with course material at any point down the track.

Training is self paced to suit the learning style of the student.

Interactivity is enhanced as all students participate, not only the most vocal or knowledgeable.

The internet is a rich environment that can simulate 'learning by doing' safely, and can incorporate activities, games and videos very easily for an active learning approach.

Onpne training is cheaper than traditional classroom based teaching making it more accessible and affordable.

Onpne training modules are developed very quickly to allow fast roll out of new concepts, changes in Government legislation and updated knowledge bases.

Onpne training automates the reporting and marking process and streampnes workflows for learning managers. Reports can be tailored to suit organisational specific requirements and are generated automatically.

Exposure to technology for learning increases famiparity and skill with technology in the workplace.

Onpne training is better for the environment - the larger the training group size, the more environmentally friendly.
